UN GDP and Total Medal Count

The first scatter plot aims to show the correlation between the Countriesâ€™ Total GDP and their medal count. The X-axis is the GDP, and the Y-axis and their medal count.

The Second scatter plot shows the same information, but divides the data points into continents using color.

Insights:

  • In general, there is a strong correlation between GDP and total medal counts, with the R2 of 0.726
  • This correlation is the strongest in North America, with the R2 of 0.984
  • This correlation is the weakest in Europe, with the R2 of 0.698
